About Chamber

The Galax City Chamber of Commerce, proudly serving the business community for fifty years, is a cornerstone of economic development and support in the picturesque city of Galax, nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains. Over the past half-century, the Chamber has been dedicated to fostering a vibrant and thriving business environment, advocating for local enterprises, and facilitating valuable connections among business leaders. Through its commitment to the growth and success of Galax's diverse business landscape, the Chamber continues to play a pivotal role in the city's economic prosperity and community well-being. We welcome you to come join our chamber!

For fifty years, the Galax City Chamber of Commerce has been a vital supporter of local businesses in the Blue Ridge Mountains' charming city of Galax. The Chamber's dedication to fostering economic growth and community connections has made it an essential resource for business success and development.
