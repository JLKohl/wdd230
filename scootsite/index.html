Welcome to Scoots

Scoots is a motor scooter rental company located on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. We are an owner-operator company that caters to locals, walk-in tourists, motor scooter enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships.

Our company currently has two physical rental locations on Cozumel. One near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and the other located near the Terminal Puerta Maya, which is further south along the coast and caters to those that ccome to out island on the cruise lines. We have friendly and easy to rent services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs.
