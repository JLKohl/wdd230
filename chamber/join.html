Chamber of Commerce Memberships

Gold MemberShip $500/year

Gold members enjoy the highest level of access and benefits. They have exclusive entry to all networking events, featured listings in our directories, and premium advertising opportunities. Additionally, Gold members receive comprehensive business support, including free workshops, personalized consulting, and complimentary use of chamber facilities. They are also strongly represented in advocacy efforts and receive special recognition and VIP treatment at chamber events.

Silver Membership $300/year

Silver members benefit from selected access to networking events and a standard presence in our business directory and newsletters. They enjoy discounted advertising options and access to business support services at reduced rates. This includes discounted workshops, group consulting sessions, and affordable use of chamber meeting rooms. Silver members are well-represented in advocacy initiatives and participate in policy discussions.

Bronze Membership $150/year

Bronze members have limited access to networking events and a basic listing in the business directory. They receive occasional mentions in newsletters and can attend selected workshops and consulting sessions at discounted rates. Bronze members are included in basic advocacy efforts and receive discounts on chamber events and services.

Non Profit Membership (NP) Free

Non-Profit members receive benefits designed to support their unique needs. They have access to networking events to connect with local businesses and community leaders. Their organizations are listed in the business directory, and they receive mentions in newsletters. Non-Profits benefit from discounted advertising rates and access to business support services, including free workshops and group consulting. They are also supported in advocacy efforts and receive special recognition in the community.
