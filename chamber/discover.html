Information about the City of Galax

About Galax

Galax Va is an independent city in the Appalachian region of Virginia. It is known as the "Old-Time Music Capitol of the World. It is a stop on the Virgina musical heritage trail known as "The Crooked Road". Galax is also known for its traditional instrument-making including a distinctive style of Appalachian dulcimer named after the city.

The population of Galax, VA in the 2022 census was 6,730.
