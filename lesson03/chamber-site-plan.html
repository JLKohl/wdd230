Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Galax City Chamber of Commerce

The site name is based on the city I have decided to use for the project. the city of Galax, VA.

Site Purpose

  • This site will help buinsess owners connect with other owners
  • This site will list what events are happening to help further buisness in Galax.

Site Scenarios

  • Where can I find Informaiton to contact the Chamber?
  • Where can I sign up for events they are hosting?
  • What education can they give me to help me further my buisness?

Color Schema

The colors for this site will be:

  • Maroon (# 660000) used as the header and footer
  • Beige (#f9efdd) used as the background color for differant sections
  • White (#ffffff) used for the font color on marron backgrounds
  • Black (#000000) used as the font color on beige backgrouds

These colors were chose based on the colors most often used for city signs and merchindise.

Typography

For the H1 I will use Sedan SC. For all other headings I will use Inconsolata. I will use Times New Roman for the rest of the site.
